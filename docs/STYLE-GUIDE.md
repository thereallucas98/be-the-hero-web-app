# BeTheHero — Style Guide v1.0

## Identity

BeTheHero is a movement + organized platform.
The visual identity balances:

- Warmth
- Trust
- Community
- Structure

Not childish. Not cold corporate.
Modern, human, and responsible visual.

---

## Typography

### Brand (Outfit)

- **Font:** Outfit
- **Weights:** 700 (Bold) or 800 (ExtraBold)
- **Usage:** Logo, hero titles, large headings
- **Letter spacing:** -1%
- **Tailwind class:** `font-display`

### UI System (Roboto)

- **Font:** Roboto
- **Weights:** 400 (body), 500 (subheadings), 600 (buttons), 700 (medium headings)
- **Tailwind class:** `font-sans` (body default)

## Buttons

- **Primary:** Hero Orange, white text, Roboto 600, radius 12px, light shadow
- **Secondary:** White, Deep Navy border, Deep Navy text, radius 12px
- **Tertiary:** Deep Navy text, hover with underline

---

## Visual Direction

Prioritize: real photos, real people, real animals, natural light, authentic expressions.
Avoid: excessive childish illustration, loud colors, caricature aesthetics.
